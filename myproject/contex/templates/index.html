Welcome to ContEx, a newly created Hypotheses Engineering Decision Support System (HE-DSS).



The goal of the HE-DSS is to facilitate the creation and execution of continuous experimentation (CE) in the context of software prototype development through a low-code approach.
Through the creation and adaptation of so-called IFML (a UI and interaction modeling language) model the HE-DSS supports the creation of experiments to test hypotheses through user interaction feedback.
This way, small companies and startups should be enabled to speed up the product development process and reduce the economic risk of software that does not meet the customerâ€™s demands.
